My name is Lileth and Letlove is an personality defined with the aspects of love, commitment and gratitude. An alter Ego? Maybe… Something I exude? Possibly… Something that I strive to manifest? Absolutely! I believe in love, even at its greatest resistance, I trust that it is the very source of what allows us to live life with purpose. Love is something I aim to capture in every session. I am grateful for the consideration and opportunity to share in your moments. I am not here to strive for anything else but to capture those in between moments and special milestones because I know how important they are.

What you receive with Letlove is an energy that is indefinable. You will usually hear me say “love” during our session, completed with a squeal and possibly a hug. I’m a hugger, but I take no offense if you’d rather give me a high five. Adora (my fur baby) is the tickle in my heart. I love all types of music and if the beat is on, then you will find me moving to it. I enjoy playing basketball, dancing, eating, and being around company that makes me better. I am a sap when it comes to love stories and usually holding back tears when my couples recite their vows. Thank you for being here. Let’s share your moments together and turn your memories into treasures!

xxo
